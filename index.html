Joe Wills

The Personal Side

Originally from New York, my wife and I moved to Hendersonville in 2007 with our 2 young children. A year later, our third child/second son arrived. I moved to Tennessee for my career, but it has been a great decision as we have made it our new home state.

The Work Side

In 1996 I started my first job in radio in New York. In 1999 I was part of a team that launched the very first internet only radio station. After that I wrote and produced a country music countdown show, which opened the door for the move to Nashville in 2007. That year I started working for one of the record labels located in Music City. In 2018, I opened my own company called Production Content, and launched Country Music Prep. In 2020, wanting to increase my skills I attended the summer Coding Bootcamp sesssion at Vanderbilt.
